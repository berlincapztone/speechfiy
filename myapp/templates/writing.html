Writing

Here you can find activities to practice your writing skills. You can improve your writing by understanding model texts and how they're structured. The self-study lessons in this section are written and organized by English level based on the Common European Framework of Reference for languages (CEFR). There are different types of model texts, with writing tips and interactive exercises that practice the writing skills you need to do well in your studies, to get ahead at work and to communicate in English in your free time. Take our free online English test to find out which level to choose. Select your level, from A1 English level (elementary) to C1 English level (advanced), and improve your writing skills at your own speed, whenever it's convenient for you.
