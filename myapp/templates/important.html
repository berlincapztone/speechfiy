Definition and Importance

Definition

The Science of Reading is an interdisciplinary body of research that encompasses cognitive psychology, neuroscience, education, linguistics, and more. It seeks to understand the processes involved in learning to read and the best instructional practices to facilitate reading development.

Learn More

Importance

Reading is fundamental to academic success and lifelong learning. Understanding the science behind how reading works can lead to more effective teaching methods, higher literacy rates, and better educational outcomes. Literacy is also linked to economic success, personal well-being, and active citizenship.
